Google Form Questions (copy/paste)

Make a Google Form and use these questions. I wrote them so you get everything you need for a clean bracket + hype intros.

1 Player / Gamertag name (exact spelling)
2 Epic ID (or platform ID) + platform (PC / PS / Xbox / Switch)
3 Region (NA / EU / OCE / SAM / ME / ASIA)
4 Time zone + best availability window
5 Discord username (required for match coordination)
6 Twitch / YouTube / TikTok links (optional but recommended)
7 Freestyle specialty (Resets / Ground / Ceiling / Pinches / Musty / Creative)
8 Your signature shot (describe it in 1 sentence)
9 Top 3 clips (links) OR one highlight montage link
10 Favorite car + decal / wheels (for your intro card)
11 Fun fact about you (keep it PG)
12 What’s your “walkout song” vibe? (genre or 1 song)
13 Do you agree to be featured on stream / socials if available? (Yes/No)
14 Are you okay with match replays being used in edits? (Yes/No)
15 Anything the host should know? (notes, constraints, etc.)
