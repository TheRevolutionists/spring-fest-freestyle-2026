Format

Shot Rules

  • 10 shots per match
  • 25 second timer
  • Freestyle judged after attempts

Close Calls

  • If it seems close, replay review will be used after the match
  • If no shots are picked by either player, it goes to a 3 shot OT
  • Final match is Best of 3

Bracket + Bounties

  • 16 player bracket
  • $25 bounty on each side of bracket
  • Bracket shown when finalized

Prize Pool

1st Place

Special Awards

Most Resets

$25 for the player with the most resets.

Highest Rated Shot

$50 for the highest rated shot of the event.

Tie Rule

If an award is tied, the money will be split between the tied players.

Judging Criteria

Creativity

Original ideas, unique setups, and variety matter.

Difficulty

Harder mechanics, cleaner execution, and tougher reads score higher.

Execution

Control and cleanliness matter. A scuffed finish can drop the score.
