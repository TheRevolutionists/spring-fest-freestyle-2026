Judges

Judges will help score each freestyle battle fairly using creativity, difficulty, execution, and overall quality. Final judge details will be posted before the event.

  • Difficulty
  • Clean Execution

Review Rules

  • No bias by popularity or region
  • Keep scoring fair and consistent

Event Notes
